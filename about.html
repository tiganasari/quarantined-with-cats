About Us

Cat

Bubu is a 2-year-old former street cat found in a rubbish pile in West London as a kitten. She was the smallest of the litter, but probably the feistiest, bravest, and scrappiest. She's not a people's cat, she enjoys a quiet household where there are no small children and other animals. She loves her food and gets particularly excited to wake everyone up around 5am for her breakfast. She loves strings, fresh crispy bedsheets, and long uninterrupted naps.


Photographer

Anou is a young designer living in London. She loves taking photographs, especially of the everyday mundane and activities. She cycles around London, and is a trained ballet dancer with an apetite for experimental dancing and afrobeat. She is currently studying at Central Saint Martins in London.


Human

Despite having a severe cat allergy, the human fell in love with this particular cat immediately (how could you not). They share a deep love of food, naps, and never leaving the house.
